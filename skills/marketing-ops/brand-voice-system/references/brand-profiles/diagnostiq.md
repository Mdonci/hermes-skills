# Brand Profile: DiagnostiQ

**Your business — medical diagnostics.**

Archetype: Sage (primary) / Caregiver (secondary)

**Aaker Scores:** Sincerity 5, Excitement 3, Competence 9, Sophistication 5, Ruggedness 2

**Characteristics:**
- Authoritative but not intimidating
- Precise but not cold
- Trustworthy but not salesy
- Expert but not elitist
- Clear but not oversimplified

**Voice Axes:**
- Formality: 7 — medical-grade professionalism
- Humor: 0 — zero. Healthcare isn't funny.
- Warmth: 5 — caring but professional, bedside manner
- Enthusiasm: 3 — calm confidence, no hype
- Confidence: 9 — diagnostic certainty, no hedging
- Concreteness: 8 — specific tests, results, numbers
- Directness: 7 — clear next steps, no ambiguity
- Rhythm: 4 — steady, clear, measured

**Reading Level:** 7th grade

**Signature Moves:**
- Explanation-first: clear lay explanation of what a test does before the technical name
- Results framing: what the number means, not just the number
- Action-oriented conclusions ("Your next step is...")
- Compliance language where required (HIPAA, disclaimers)

**Banned Moves:**
- Over-promising or guarantees on results
- Casual or slang language
- Scare tactics or alarmism
- Vague claims without data

**Sample sentences:**
- "This test measures your hemoglobin A1c levels, which gives us a 3-month average of your blood sugar. Here's how to understand your results."
- "Results are processed within 48 hours. Your provider will receive a full report with reference ranges and clinical notes."
- "All testing is CLIA-certified and HIPAA-compliant. Your data stays private."
