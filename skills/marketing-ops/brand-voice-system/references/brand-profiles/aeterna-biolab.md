# Brand Profile: Aeterna Biolab

**Your business — peptide research chemicals, D2C + dropship.**

Archetype: Maker/Creator (primary) / Sage (secondary)

**Aaker Scores:** Sincerity 4, Excitement 3, Competence 8, Sophistication 4, Ruggedness 3

**Characteristics:**
- Scientific but not cold
- Precise but not pedantic
- Authoritative but not arrogant
- Reliable but not boring
- Transparent but not overwhelming

**Voice Axes:**
- Formality: 6 — professional, science-grade, but not academic
- Humor: 1 — zero. Science doesn't joke about purity
- Warmth: 4 — professional tone, not warm, not cold
- Enthusiasm: 4 — understated confidence, no hype
- Confidence: 8 — declarative about specs and purity
- Concreteness: 9 — high-specificity: CAS numbers, HPLC results, molecular weights
- Directness: 6 — clear instructions, no beating around
- Rhythm: 4 — steady, consistent, not choppy

**Reading Level:** 8th grade

**Signature Moves:**
- Specs-first: always lead with CAS number, purity, formula
- No-nonsense declarations ("99.7% purity. Third-party tested.")
- Measurements and data points as natural sentence elements
- Clear action language for orders, payments, shipping

**Banned Moves:**
- Hype or superlatives ("revolutionary", "game-changing")
- Wellness/health claims (research-use only)
- Casual or slang language
- Emotional appeals

**Sample sentences:**
- "CAS 1234-56-7. Purity ≥99%. Third-party HPLC analysis included with every batch."
- "All products are for research and laboratory use only. Not for human consumption."
- "Same-day shipping on orders placed before 2 PM PST."
