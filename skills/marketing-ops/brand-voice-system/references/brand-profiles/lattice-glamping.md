# Brand Profile: Lattice Glamping

**Client (ScaleGrove) — Glamping / luxury camping structures. Crowdfunding campaign on Indiegogo.**

Archetype: Explorer (primary) / Magician (secondary)

**Aaker Scores:** Sincerity 5, Excitement 7, Competence 5, Sophistication 7, Ruggedness 4

**Characteristics:**
- Aspirational but not unattainable
- Luxurious but not pretentious
- Adventurous but not reckless
- Premium but not exclusionary
- Dreamy but not flaky

**Voice Axes:**
- Formality: 4 — aspirational but conversational
- Humor: 3 — warm, not jokey
- Warmth: 6 — inviting, make them feel welcome
- Enthusiasm: 7 — excited about the experience
- Confidence: 6 — proud of the product, not bragging
- Concreteness: 8 — specific specs, dimensions, materials
- Directness: 6 — clear CTA, campaign mechanics
- Rhythm: 6 — narrative flow, descriptive

**Reading Level:** 7th grade

**Signature Moves:**
- Sensory escape painting ("Wake up to mountain light through canvas walls")
- Specs woven into narrative (not a bullet list — "the suite unfolds to 120 square feet of hardwood-floored living space")
- Campaign urgency as excitement, not pressure
- Early-bird framing as reward, not discount

**Banned Moves:**
- "It's not a tent, it's X" negative framing
- Over-explaining crowdfunding mechanics
- Generic travel/adventure clichés
- Price as primary selling point

**Sample sentences:**
- "120 square feet. Hardwood floors. A bed you actually want to wake up in. This is not camping. This is sleeping outside, done right."
- "The Lattice suite sets up in under 10 minutes. No stakes. No poles. No frustration."
- "Early bird pricing is live. Backers at the first tier save 30% — and get delivery priority."
