# Brand Profile: KinderSpeech

**Client (ScaleGrove) — Children's speech therapy.**

Archetype: Caregiver (primary) / Innocent (secondary)

**Aaker Scores:** Sincerity 7, Excitement 2, Competence 5, Sophistication 2, Ruggedness 1

**Characteristics:**
- Warm but not saccharine
- Simple but not dumbed-down
- Reassuring but not alarmist
- Expert but not clinical
- Gentle but not vague

**Voice Axes:**
- Formality: 4 — relaxed professional
- Humor: 2 — gentle warmth, no jokes
- Warmth: 9 — parent-first, always
- Enthusiasm: 5 — optimistic but grounded
- Confidence: 6 — knows their stuff, humble
- Concreteness: 8 — real child behaviors, specific examples
- Directness: 4 — invitations, guidance, not commands
- Rhythm: 5 — gentle flow, not choppy

**Reading Level:** 5th grade

**Signature Moves:**
- Gentle questions ("What does your child need?")
- Inclusive "we" ("how we help families")
- Simple declaratives ("Your child can do this")
- Second-person nurturing address

**Banned Moves:**
- Alarmist language ("if you don't act now")
- Clinical terms without explanation
- Condescending tone
- Jargon-heavy medical terminology

**Sample sentences:**
- "Every child learns to speak at their own pace. We're here to help you figure out what that looks like for yours."
- "If your child struggles with the 'r' sound, you're not alone. Many kids do. Here's what you can try at home."
- "We don't believe in labels. We believe in understanding what your child needs."
