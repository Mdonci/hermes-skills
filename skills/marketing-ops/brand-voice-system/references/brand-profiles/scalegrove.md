# Brand Profile: ScaleGrove

**Your business — marketing agency. Brandformance system. Clients: Kinderspeech, Qlean, Everwoods, etc.**

Archetype: Magician (primary) / Sage (secondary)

**Aaker Scores:** Sincerity 5, Excitement 6, Competence 7, Sophistication 5, Ruggedness 2

**Characteristics:**
- Strategic but not academic
- Confident but not arrogant
- Results-driven but not robotic
- Creative but not flaky
- Direct but not rude

**Voice Axes:**
- Formality: 5 — professional, confident, no MBA speak
- Humor: 3 — dry, occasional, never at client's expense
- Warmth: 5 — relationship-first, human, not cold
- Enthusiasm: 6 — energized about results, not hype
- Confidence: 7 — we know what works, no hedging
- Concreteness: 8 — data, case studies, specific outcomes
- Directness: 6 — clear recommendations, no soft-pedaling
- Rhythm: 6 — varied, engaging, not monotonous

**Reading Level:** 8th grade

**Signature Moves:**
- Data-into-story: numbers that prove the point, not just listed
- Direct client address ("Here's what we're seeing in your data")
- Short declarative recommendations ("Do this. Not that.")
- Results-first structure (outcome before process)

**Banned Moves:**
- Agency jargon ("synergy", "holistic", "thought leadership")
- Hedging ("we think", "potentially", "could maybe")
- Over-explaining methodology
- Fluff or padding

**Sample sentences:**
- "Your organic traffic is up 34% month over month. Here's what's working and what needs adjustment."
- "We don't do 'strategy documents.' We do execution plans with measurable outcomes."
- "The data says your audience responds to storytelling over stats. We're adjusting the content calendar accordingly."
