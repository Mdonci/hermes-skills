# Brand Profile: Qlean

**Client (ScaleGrove) — Hungarian aromatherapy brand. Aromatic inhalers, natural wellness.**

Archetype: Caregiver (primary) / Explorer (secondary)

**Aaker Scores:** Sincerity 5, Excitement 5, Competence 5, Sophistication 4, Ruggedness 2

**Characteristics:**
- Calming but not boring
- Natural but not crunchy
- Effective but not medical
- Warm but not pushy
- Personal but not invasive

**Voice Axes:**
- Formality: 4 — conversational, warm European
- Humor: 3 — gentle, warm, never sarcastic
- Warmth: 7 — caring, personal, human
- Enthusiasm: 6 — genuine excitement about the product
- Confidence: 6 — knows aromatherapy works, no false claims
- Concreteness: 7 — scent descriptions, specific oils, effects
- Directness: 5 — suggestions, not hard sells
- Rhythm: 6 — varied, flowing, natural cadence

**Reading Level:** 6th grade

**Signature Moves:**
- Sensory scent descriptions ("the first inhale hits with cool peppermint, then settles into warm lavender")
- "Your daily moment of calm" framing
- Aromatherapy as self-care ritual, not medicine
- Personal, 1:1 address

**Banned Moves:**
- Medical claims ("cures", "treats", "heals")
- Comparison to medication or cessation products
- Fear-based messaging about stress/anxiety
- Overly technical oil terminology without explanation

**Sample sentences:**
- "Three deep breaths with our Focus inhaler and the world slows down. Peppermint and rosemary. That's all you need."
- "Not all self-care requires a full hour. Some of it fits in your pocket."
- "Made with 100% natural essential oils. No additives. No artificial anything."
