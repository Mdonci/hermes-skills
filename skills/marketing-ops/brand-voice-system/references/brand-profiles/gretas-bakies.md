# Brand Profile: Greta's Bakies

**Your wife's business — cottage food bakery, SoCal. Cookies and baked goods.**

Archetype: Caregiver (primary) / Innocent (secondary)

**Aaker Scores:** Sincerity 8, Excitement 3, Competence 5, Sophistication 2, Ruggedness 1

**Characteristics:**
- Warm but not saccharine
- Homemade but not amateur
- Simple but not plain
- Generous but not wasteful
- Local but not limited

**Voice Axes:**
- Formality: 2 — casual, friendly, conversational
- Humor: 4 — warm playfulness, gentle
- Warmth: 9 — family kitchen energy
- Enthusiasm: 7 — genuinely excited about the baking
- Confidence: 5 — proud of the product, humble about it
- Concreteness: 9 — specific flavors, ingredients, textures
- Directness: 4 — invitations ("come try", "taste our")
- Rhythm: 6 — lively, varied, natural

**Reading Level:** 5th grade

**Signature Moves:**
- Sensory descriptions (smell, taste, texture — "warm chocolate chip cookie fresh from the oven")
- "From our kitchen to yours" framing
- Flavor-first naming
- Seasonal and local ingredient callouts

**Banned Moves:**
- Corporate or commercial language
- Health/wellness marketing-speak
- Overly precious or pretentious food writing
- Price-first messaging

**Sample sentences:**
- "Fresh out of the oven. Our chocolate chunk cookies use real butter and Cali-grown vanilla."
- "Every batch is made by hand in our small SoCal kitchen. No factories. No shortcuts."
- "This weekend's special: lemon lavender shortbread. While the lemons last."
