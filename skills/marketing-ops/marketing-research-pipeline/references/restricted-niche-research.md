# Restricted Niche Research Patterns

> Captured from Qlean.hu research session (2026-05-18)

## The Niche Pattern

Products that look like vapes but claim to be aromatherapy/wellness devices face a unique marketing challenge: **Meta's strict ban on tobacco/nicotine/vape ads** means paid social is unreliable or impossible. The fallback is owned channels: email, SMS, community, referral.

### Meta Policy Details
- Strict global ban on tobacco, nicotine, vape pens, e-cigarettes, vaporizers
- Automated moderation flags products that *look like* vapes even if they contain no nicotine
- 2025 sensitive category changes restrict tracking/optimization for flagged brands
- Aromatherapy devices exist in a gray area — MONQ got an FDA warning, not a Meta ban, but individual ad accounts get restricted

### What to avoid in copy (triggers restrictions)
- Vape, vaping, vaporizer, e-cig, e-cigarette
- Nicotine, tobacco, smoke, smoking, cigarette
- "Quit smoking," "stop smoking," "smoking cessation"
- Addiction, withdrawal, cravings (clinical claims without FDA backing)
- Health claims: "treats," "cures," "prevents," "reduces risk"

### Safe framing language (Hungarian)
- "Természetes alternatíva" (Natural alternative)
- "Tudatos szokások" (Conscious habits)
- "Frissítő aromaterápia" (Refreshing aromatherapy)
- "100% növényi alapú illóolajok"
- "Váltás a természetesre"
- "Támogatja a pozitív változást"

### FÜM's Brand Formula (steal this)
- "Same you. Better habits." → Identity-preserving transformation
- "Zero nicotine, vapor or batteries" → Defuses the vape comparison directly
- "Clinically studied, lab tested" → Trust without health claims
- "1/3 Cost of Your Bad Habit" → Price anchoring against old habit
- Position as a **habit replacement** not a **quit tool**
- Focus on what you *gain* (calm, freshness, natural) not what you *quit*

## Email Strategy for Restricted Niches

### Key Leverage Points
1. **Subscription is the business model** — consumable product + no ad channel = subscription is not optional, it's survival. FÜM's Cores Club at 30% off + free shipping + private FB group is the template.
2. **Lead capture everywhere** — exit popup, discount, quiz ("which flavor fits you?"), blog CTA, post-purchase nudge
3. **Welcome sequence is the primary conversion funnel** since you can't run Meta retargeting
4. **Referral program** drives acquisition when ads can't
5. **Community (private FB group)** becomes the organic acquisition engine
6. **Deliverability is critical** — set up domain auth (SPF/DKIM/DMARC) immediately, avoid flagged keywords in subject lines

### Platform Recommendation
- **ActiveCampaign** for restricted niches (more tolerant, Hungarian-friendly, GDPR-compliant, affordable)
- **Klaviyo** as alternative (stronger e-commerce features but may flag nicotine-adjacent products)

### LTV Multiplier
Without subscription: ~€96/year (if customer remembers to reorder 12x)
With subscription: ~€288/year (guaranteed 12x auto-orders) = **3x LTV**
