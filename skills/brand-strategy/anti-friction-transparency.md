## About This Skill

Anti-Friction Transparency is a methodology that uses process transparency as a risk reversal mechanism. The core principle: when buyers can see exactly how things work, objections dissolve before they form. This is GTM strategy, not just sales support.

## Key Concepts

- **Objection Flipping**: Proactively stating the objection and reframing it as evidence of fit — done in content, not in sales calls
- **Process Transparency**: Showing exactly how the work happens, including timelines, failure modes, and handling procedures — before the buyer asks
- **Anti-Friction Pack**: 10-page document with 10 most common objections and their Brandformance sentence flips — primary lead magnet for Marketing Directors
- **Risk Reversal Through Transparency**: The more visible the process, the less perceived risk in the purchase decision

## How to Use This Skill

Use when a client's conversion rate is suffering from repeated objections in the sales process. Build the Anti-Friction Pack as a lead magnet and reference it proactively in content. The goal: buyers arrive at sales calls already pre-sold on the objection handling because they read the pack.
