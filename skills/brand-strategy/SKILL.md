# Brand Strategy Skills

## Overview

Brand strategy skills grounded in the Brandformance System and 4321 Content Flywheel methodology. These skills enable building durable owned authority assets through unified brand philosophy and high-velocity conversion logic.

---

## Core Frameworks

### Brandformance System

**Description:** A unified approach combining brand philosophy with conversion logic. Creates durable owned authority assets.

**Origin:** David Mankovich

**Components:**
- Insight Builder methodology
- Message Architecture Canvas (MAC)
- Transformation Map
- Trust Funnel Stages (Month 1-3 progression)

**Key Principle:** Market dominance belongs to the 'Strategic Architect' who unifies brand philosophy with high-velocity conversion logic.

**Usage:** Core methodology throughout 90-day roadmap

---

### Mosaic Brand Strategy

**Description:** Brand is an entire operational thing, not just a logo. $100M+ revenue impact retrospective.

**Origin:** Lara Acosta

**Week Referenced:** Week 9

**Components:**
- Brand as operational system
- Metrics define authority
- Enterprise-wide brand coherence

**Tactical Pattern:** Retellability Test — 15-second retellable story test. If they can't repeat it, it's not a brand.

---

### SLAY Framework

**Description:** Visual rhythm for mobile-first content (60-70% mobile dwell time). Maximizes engagement on mobile devices.

**Origin:** Lara Acosta

**Components:**
- **S_story:** Start with 8-10 word hook
  - Example: "I asked 100 founders why their ads were failing."
- **L_lesson:** One sharp insight
  - Example: "Ad creative is a symptom; broken buying logic is the disease."
- **A_actionable:** Give them a tool
  - Example: "Use the Transformation Map to find your Snap Point before you open Meta Ads."
- **Y_you_engagement:** Ask a polarizing question
  - Example: "Is your brand narrative easy for a customer to repeat? Tell me below."

**Tactical Patterns:**
- hook-first writing
- modular insight delivery
- tool-based CTAs
- engagement prompting

## Methods & Techniques

### Trust Funnel Stages

**Description:** 3-month progression from legitimacy to high-ticket closes.

| Stage | Focus | Deliverable |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| Month 1 | Legitimacy and Credibility | Foundation |
| Month 2 | Product Belief and Value Alignment | Systematization |
| Month 3 | Universal Belief in You (high-ticket closes) | Strategic Architect |

---

### Insight Builder

**Description:** Stop inventing messages; start collecting behaviors. Behavioral research method.

**Week Referenced:** Week 1

**Principle:** Messages should be discovered through observation, not invented in a vacuum.

---

### Champion Targeting

**Description:** Why brands fail when trying to speak to everyone. Defining the ideal customer.

**Principle:** Focus on the economic buyer obsessed with gross margins and the marketing director drowning in attribution complexity.

---

### Executive Content Engine

**Description:** Treating visibility as a structural requirement.

**Case Study:** ROSSMANN — awareness from 11% to 80%

**Principle:** Executive visibility is infrastructure, not vanity.

---

## Tactical Patterns

1. **Mobile-First Visual Rhythm:** SLAY framework for 60-70% mobile dwell time optimization
2. **Retellability Test:** 15-second story test — if they can't repeat it, it's not a brand
3. **Story First Selling:** People buy stories first, evidence second
4. **High-Ticket Trust Signals:** Awards, case studies, valuation moves as credibility indicators
