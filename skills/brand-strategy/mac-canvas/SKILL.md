## About This Skill

The Message Architecture Canvas (MAC) provides a systematic approach to brand messaging: stop inventing messages, start collecting behaviors. The Snap Point identifies when buyer tolerance for the current workaround collapses. The Transformation Map charts the journey from untested messaging to predictable growth.

## Key Concepts

- **Behavior Collection**: Messages come from market interactions, not invention
- **Snap Point**: When buyer's tolerance for current workaround collapses — conversion trigger
- **Transformation Map**: Journey from untested messaging to predictable growth
- **Structural Differentiation**: Cross-vertical experience as moat that generalists can't replicate
- **MAC System**: Iterative messaging architecture, not one-time exercise

## How to Use This Skill

Apply MAC by collecting behaviors — what clients say, how they describe problems, when they decide to buy. The Snap Point is the moment they can no longer tolerate their current approach. Map the transformation from their starting state to your solution.

Cross-vertical experience is often overlooked as a differentiator. 15 years across multiple industries creates pattern recognition no specialist can match. Use MAC to systemize your messaging, then iterate based on market response.
