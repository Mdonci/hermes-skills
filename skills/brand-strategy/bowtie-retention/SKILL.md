## About This Skill

The Bowtie Funnel concept shifts focus from acquisition to retention as the primary growth driver. The critical insight: 50-70% of growth in 2026 comes from retention. The fundamental principle — Brand drives Performance, but Performance does not Drive Brand — reorients strategy toward brand equity as the compound asset.

## Key Concepts

- **Bowtie Funnel**: 50-70% of growth from retention, creating the characteristic bowtie shape
- **Brand > Performance**: Strong brand creates performance; strong performance doesn't create brand
- **Price Commoditization**: Competing on price leads to race to bottom
- **Story Survival**: Brands that win on story protect margins; those on price get commoditized

## How to Use This Skill

Shift your strategic focus from constant acquisition to retention and brand building. The Bowtie Funnel reminds us that customer lifetime value exceeds first-purchase value many times over. Build systems that deepen existing relationships, not just fill the top of the funnel.

Internalize the principle: brand drives performance. Good performance from good brand. But good brand doesn't automatically follow from good performance. Invest in brand equity as the compound asset that compounds over customer lifetime and across acquisition cycles.
