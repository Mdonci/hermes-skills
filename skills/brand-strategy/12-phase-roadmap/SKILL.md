## About This Skill

The 12-Phase Roadmap provides a complete year-long LinkedIn strategy built from 3 years of research, analyzing thousands of posts, and working with top creators. The guarantee: minimum 50,000 new followers per year plus extra six figures annual income. The system works because it's framework-based, not algorithm-dependent.

## Key Concepts

- **12 Phases**: Sequential strategy building on previous phases throughout the year
- **Guarantee**: 50,000 followers/year + six figures additional income for those who execute
- **Framework vs Tactics**: Content works regardless of algorithm — it's built on principles, not hacks
- **3 Years of Research**: Analyzed thousands of posts, worked with top creators

## How to Use This Skill

Commit to the full 12-phase roadmap. Each phase builds on the last — skipping creates gaps in the foundation. The consistency requirement is non-negotiable: showing up weekly for 52 weeks is the minimum. Focus on the framework — why content works — not just what to post.

Results compound over time. The 270,000 followers and millions of views came from 3 years of systematic execution, not viral posts. Trust the system, execute consistently, measure what matters.
