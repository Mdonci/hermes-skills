## About This Skill

The Retellability Test is a Brandformance framework for evaluating whether your brand narrative has achieved the minimum threshold of memorability to function as an actual brand asset. If a customer cannot repeat your core story to a colleague in 15 seconds, it is not yet a brand — it is merely content.

## Key Concepts

- **15-Second Rule**: The maximum complexity threshold for brand-level memorability — anything more complex requires a conversation, not a retelling
- **POV Storytelling**: Format where reader becomes focal point — "POV, you [aspiration]" — creates immediate self-identification
- **Human Truth**: The universal emotional experience at the center of your brand (not a product feature or service category)
- **Repetition Loop**: Brand memorability requires the same message delivered across multiple contexts until the neural pathway is established

## How to Use This Skill

Use this when a client has great content but no brand equity. Run the 15-second test before any campaign launch. If the story fails, rebuild using the POV format and human truth extraction. The goal is always: can this survive the game of telephone across a conference hallway?
