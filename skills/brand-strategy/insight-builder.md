## About This Skill

Insight Builder is a behavioral research methodology that replaces message invention with message discovery. The core principle: messages should be found in the observable behaviors of your target audience, not invented in a strategist's imagination.

## Key Concepts

- **Behavioral Research**: Systematic observation of what your audience actually says, does, and fears — not what they might say in a survey
- **Snap Point**: The moment a buyer's tolerance for their current workaround collapses, creating maximum urgency for change
- **Message Emergence**: Letting the pattern reveal itself from collected data rather than forcing a message into existence
- **SMPV Framework**: Scale → Market → Problem → Value; a tool for organizing discovered insights into clear positioning

## How to Use This Skill

Use when a client says "I don't know what to say" or "our messaging isn't working." The first step is never writing — it's research. Send the client on a 2-week observation sprint to collect behavioral data before any message creation begins. The message will reveal itself if you look hard enough.
