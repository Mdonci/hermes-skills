## About This Skill

Executive Content Engine is a methodology for treating executive visibility as structural infrastructure rather than optional marketing activity. The core principle: in a world where content is the primary trust-building mechanism, an invisible executive is an invisible company.

## Key Concepts

- **Visibility as Infrastructure**: Like city infrastructure — invisible when present, catastrophic when absent; the absence of executive visibility creates a trust vacuum competitors will fill
- **ROSSMANN Case Study**: 11% to 80% brand awareness achieved through executive visibility strategy, not paid media campaign
- **Executive Content Engine**: Systematic approach to positioning executive perspective as the primary trust-building asset — 1-2 quality posts per week vs 5 mediocre ones
- **Multi-Channel Visibility Stack**: Social posting + speaking + podcasts + earned media + published thought leadership — all working together to establish visible executive presence

## How to Use This Skill

Use when a client (especially founder or C-suite) believes they can defer visibility work until later. Show them the ROSSMANN data. Make clear: the 6-12 month build period means delay is not neutral — it is active erosion of competitive position. The question is not whether to build visibility infrastructure but when to start.
