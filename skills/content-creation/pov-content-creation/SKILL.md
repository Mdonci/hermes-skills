description: Create a week-long POV content strategy using once-weekly storytelling posts paired with educational follow-up content.
trigger: "When planning a weekly content calendar that uses POV storytelling format"
steps:
  - Publish one POV story post per week (LinkedIn or social platform of choice)
  - Use the POV post to show transformation or aspirational scenario — make it emotional
  - Follow up mid-week with educational content that explains the step-by-step of what was shown
  - Test and iterate your POV posts based on engagement signals
  - Track what themes/resonances work and refine future POV executions
pitfalls:
  - Using the same exact story repeatedly — audiences get bored
  - Skipping the educational follow-up that adds unique value beyond the story
  - Not iterating based on performance data
  - Overusing the format — balance POV with other content types
examples:
  - |
    Monday: POV post about "You just built a 7-figure business and your life is 10x better"
    Wednesday: Educational post breaking down exactly how you built it step by step
  - |
    Once a week on LinkedIn keeps the format fresh while allowing testing and iteration
