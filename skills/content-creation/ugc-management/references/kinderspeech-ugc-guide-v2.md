## Key Innovations

1. **Anti-Formula Section** — explicitly declares no templates, explains why the old approach backfired
2. **10 DO's + 10 DON'Ts** — specific, behavioral, testable rules with CEE examples
3. **The Lamp Analogy** — product should exist in frame like a lamp (present, illuminates, not the subject)
4. **4-Question Test** — structural (Q1), opening (Q2), emotional (Q3), audio (Q4) — catches failures before posting
5. **One Narrow Exception** — verbal reference allowed only as child-directed natural mention
6. **Caption-Only CTA** — features, guarantee, shipping, ebook go in caption, never in video
