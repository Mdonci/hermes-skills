# Lead Magnets

## Overview

Lead magnets are rotating assets that move "rented" attention (social media followers) to "owned" list (email/audience). The 4321 Content Flywheel designates 1 lead magnet per cycle to systematically grow owned authority.

## Lead Magnet Strategy

### Rotation Cadence

The 4321 Content Flywheel designates 1 rotating lead magnet per cycle:

| Cycle | Lead Magnet Focus |
|-------|-------------------|
| Month 1 | Anti-Friction Pack (credibility) |
| Month 2 | MAC Template + AI Support Stack (value) |
| Month 3 | Fractional CMO Playbook (high-ticket) |

### Purpose

- Move "rented" attention (social followers) to "owned" list (email)
- Systematically grow owned authority
- Create follow-up touchpoints beyond content consumption
- Enable segmentation by lead magnet type

## Lead Magnet Creation Patterns

### Pattern 1: Anti-Friction Pack

**Format:** 10-Page PDF

**Structure:**
1. Title: [Problem] — The Brandformance Solution
2. Introduction: Why [Industry] buyers hesitate
3. Objection 1-10: Common objection → Brandformance sentence that flips it
4. CTA: Book strategy call

**Tone:** Direct, transparent, slightly contrarian

---

### Pattern 2: MAC Template

**Format:** Interactive Sheet/GPT

**Structure:**
1. Brand fundamentals section
2. Functional vs Structural differentiation
3. Transformation mapping
4. Hero Line builder
5. Export to messaging playbook

---

### Pattern 3: Snap Point Checklist

**Format:** Single-page checklist

**Structure:**
1. Current state assessment (5 questions)
2. Scoring guide
3. Interpretation (green/yellow/red)
4. Recommended action based on score
5. CTA for deeper work

---

### Pattern 4: Video Walkthrough (Loom)

**Format:** 5-10 min screen recording

**Structure:**
1. Hook: Problem statement (30 sec)
2. Solution walkthrough: Show the system (5-7 min)
3. Results: Specific numbers achieved (1 min)
4. CTA: Get full template / Book call (30 sec)

---

### Pattern 5: Course/Delayed Automation

**Format:** Email sequence + content modules

**Structure:**
1. Welcome + orientation
2. Module 1-7 delivered daily
3. Case study module
4. Transformation milestone
5. Offer placement

## Distribution Strategy

### Content Integration
- Each lead magnet ties to specific week/theme in 90-day roadmap
- Social posts reference lead magnets as "download" or "get the template"
- Story content teases lead magnet; Educate content provides preview; Sell content delivers CTA

### Email Integration
- New subscribers receive appropriate lead magnet based on opt-in source
- Follow-up sequence tied to lead magnet topic
- Segmentation: Founder leads vs Marketing Director leads

### Retargeting Integration
- Content viewers → Lead magnet retargeting
- Lead magnet downloaders → High-ticket offer sequence
- Email subscribers → Cross-sell other lead magnets
